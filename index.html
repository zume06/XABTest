Musical Instrument Sound Similarity Test

Thank you for your cooperation in my experiment.
This experiment is musical instrument sound similarity test.
I will explain the experimental procedure.

Experimental Procedure

  1. Enter your name and select the set list number 1 or 2.
  2. Press "Start Experiment" button.
  3. First, listen to the instrument sound at the top (let's call it Sound X).
    Next, listen to the two sounds below SoundX and choose the one that you think is closer to Sound X.
    If you are not sure, choose "~ might be closer to X".
    Be sure to listen to all instrumental sounds all the way through.
  4. Press "next" button and start next evaluation.
  5. When you finish this experiment, press "finish" button and download the result.
    Send it to slack or the following email address.
  6. Email address: hashizume.yuuka@g.sp.m.is.nagoya-u.ac.jp

Notes

  • This experiment takes about 15 minutes per set..
  • Please do not update the page during this experiment.
  • You may not be able to download the results on your phone, so please use your PC.
  • Please use headphones for this experiment.

実験手順

  5. 評価実験を全て終えたら"finish"ボタンを押して結果をダウンロードして下さい。
